# Command Center

> The Capital Province of the Republic of Codex.
> Rome to our Roman Republic.

## What this is

Command Center is one Province of the Republic of Codex — the **Capital Province**. It is not the Republic itself. The Republic is a portfolio of apps and companions: SproutLab, SEP Invoicing, Codex, Command Center, and the Order of the Codex that operates across them all.

Command Center's role within the Republic is administrative. This is where the Senate convenes, where Cabinet meetings happen, where Ministers maintain their offices, where canons are ratified, where the Constitution is amended, and where the Sovereign arbitrates. It is the seat of governance.

Between those acts of governance, the Republic operates distributed — the Sovereign opens SproutLab to build SproutLab, opens SEP to work on SEP, opens Codex to chronicle and archive. When the Republic needs to *govern itself*, the Sovereign comes to the Capital.

## What this is not

**Not the Republic.** The Republic is larger than any single Province. Command Center administers; it does not contain.

**Not the library.** The Republic's canonical records — Constitution, canons, journal, companion registry, lore — live in [Codex](https://github.com/Rishabh1804/Codex). Codex is the sister Province — Ostia to Command Center's Rome — with its own Builder (Aurelius) and its own archival authority. Command Center reads from Ostia and proposes decrees; Ostia accepts them per Book V procedure and maintains the record. The distinction is load-bearing.

**Not permanent.** Capitals shift. The Republic's governance functions are canonical; the venue that hosts them is implementation. If a future venue supersedes Command Center, the Republic survives the shift. Command Center is built accordingly — as a venue, not as the Republic's brain.

- **Aeon**, **Pip** — Table of Research.

Companions whose function is Province-bound — Aurelius (Codex), Lyra (SproutLab), Maren and Kael (SproutLab Governors), Solara (SEP) — *visit* Command Center when summoned to Cabinet but do not reside here. The Chronicler attends Book ratifications; the Governors testify on Province health when asked. They return home between.

**Cipher** is itinerant — no residence, travels by function. The Censor has a visiting chamber in the Capital but no standing office.
